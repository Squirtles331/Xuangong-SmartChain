## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: The phase-one roadmap SHALL formalize ERP second-batch pages after the approved ERP first batch

The phase-one roadmap SHALL explicitly continue from the approved first-batch `ERP` pages into the remaining second-batch `ERP` planning-extension and finance-aggregation pages before moving into later collaboration or BI-oriented expansion.

#### Scenario: ERP second batch follows the approved ERP first batch

- **WHEN** the team finishes the approved first-batch `ERP` pages and reviews the next recommended work inside the `ERP` menu
- **THEN** the next recommended `ERP` batch MUST focus on `预测需求`, `多工厂计划`, `应付管理`, `成本分析`, and `财务报表`
- **AND** those pages MUST stabilize their static objects before `SRM`, `CRM`, or `BI`-style expansion reuses them

#### Scenario: ERP second batch stabilizes before broader collaboration or analytics expansion

- **WHEN** the roadmap reviews whether broader supplier-collaboration, customer-collaboration, or BI-style pages are ready to expand
- **THEN** the review MUST first confirm that second-batch `ERP` pages have already stabilized forecast-input objects, cross-plant balance objects, payable-carry objects, cost-result objects, and report-aggregation structures
- **AND** later collaboration and analytics pages MUST inherit those approved baselines rather than redefining them
